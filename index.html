About Me

I'm Praveen Tyagi, a passionate educator with over 30 years of experience mentoring India’s brightest minds. As the Founder and Managing Director of PACE IIT & Medical, I’ve had the privilege of helping thousands of students achieve their dreams of gaining admission into IITs, NITs, AIIMS, and top global universities like Harvard, MIT, Stanford, Cornell to name a few.


I specialize in Physics and Advanced Mathematics, known for my structured pedagogy, real-world conceptual teaching, and commitment to student success.


Now based in the U.S. for part of the year, I’m looking to extend my support to motivated, underrepresented students through tutoring, mentoring, and test prep support.

My Journey
